Excel, SQL and Power BI

The scenario is that the Head of Marketing wants to find out who the top YouTubers are in 2024 to decide on which YouTubers would be best to run marketing campaigns throughout the rest of the year. I started by obtaining and exploring the relevant data using Excel to gain a basic understanding and identify any apparent errors or patterns. I loaded the data into the SQL Server for further cleaning, which included removing duplicates, correcting datatypes, and filling in missing values. I analyzed YouTube channels with the most subscribers, total views, and videos uploaded and crosschecked Excel results with SQL outputs to check for discrepancies. Once I was confident in the data's quality and reliability, I visualized it in Power BI. This tool helped me create interactive visualizations and dashboards that highlighted key trends and insights. Based on these visualizations, I generated findings that captured the essential insights from the data, leading to informed decisions and strategies. Finally, I documented the entire process, including the methodologies, analyses, and conclusions.
